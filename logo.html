Ideation And Sketches

I began this project with research and sketches. I was particularly interested in the history of Crete in addition to the natural beauty of the countryside. Crete is well-known for its Minoan ruins as well as its uniquely Mediterranean climate, and I felt that the direction of my design should reflect that. With that in mind, I began sketching not only the ruins of the island, but also its topography. I also paid particular attention to symbols of mythology associated with the culutre of ancient Crete.

First Pass

This first pass was the culmination of several sketches as well as other assets pulled from prior projects. For example, the palm tree was from an earlier illustrator project that eventually became an after effects video. Further iterations on this design would see multiple sweeping changes, although the underlying aesthetic would remain the same.

Crete Tourism Logo

Crete is an island off of Greece located in the Mediterranean sea, known to be the most populous of the many isles dotting the area. An ancient place, Crete was an established kingdom thousands of years before the modern age, and has a deep and rich history that has inspired myths and other tales throughout ancient times. Now the island is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful landscapes and beaches, as well as the ruins dotting its landscape that contain many examples of Minoan art.

Thought Process

Although Crete is well known, I have not seen a tourist logo that I felt inspired anyone to visit it. I wanted to incorporate both the landscape and Creteâ€™s history into one image that I felt could evoke an emotion. I wanted a simple image that would inspire a feeling of beauty, but also severity using a bold typeface that demands attention. One of the challenges was colour, as I felt that blues and greens were not evocative of the tranquility I was seeking, and opted instead to choose the colour of the setting sun against the beach and water, as well as the column.
